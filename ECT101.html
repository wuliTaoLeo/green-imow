Product

The ECT101 is an AGM-powered electric stacker specifically designed to replace forklift trucks when it comes to unloading freights from trucks as well as other outdoor operation requirements. With a combination of the versatility of a forklift and benefits of a walkie stacker, ECT101 offers a one-stop solution from discharging, transporting to stacking to fit the needs of material handing professionals at each point of distribution process.

Application

Perfect for Floor Operation: With overall length of 2087mm, turning radius of 1229mm, and optimized service weight of 1280kg, ECT101 fits perfectly in a cargo lift and is able to allocate loads on higher floors and narrow alleys.

Features

Optimized Drive System

Powered by 48V motor, ECT101 can travel up at great gradeability of 6% with full load. The improved heat dissipation designed controller prevents the truck from getting overheated.

Upgraded hydraulic system

The hydraulic system is now with low energy loss and fine appearance. The chromium coating cylinder of high hardness is not easy to get rusty and keeps fine appearance.

Brilliant Visibility

The low chassis of ECT101 ensures good visibility during operation. This makes it a safe machine to use when in confined spaces.

Easy Handling

The long-sized handle makes steering operation much easier. The bottom of steering handle is equipped with water-proofed cables that is able to prevent electricity leakage and ensure you operation safety.
